ABOUT NEW JERSEY

New Jersey (NJ), located in the northeastern region, is one of the 50 states of The United States Of America, specifically, the 3rd state in Order Of Statehood. New Jersey is divided in 21 counties and 564 municipalities. Disperse in all 21 counties, there are 9.29 million people living in NJ.

The rural areas of NJ are the most visited because of it's diversity in plants, flowers, and animals. NJ is also known as the State Of Flowers.
On top of that, there are many states around NJ including: New York, Pennsylvania, Delaware, Connecticut, and Maryland.
